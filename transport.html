AIR FREIGHT SERVICES

Our extensive air fleet, global network and strong relationships with major commercial airlines mean that we are well-positioned to provide a wide range of aviation logistics services.

The delivery of freight requires a secure, fast, flexible transport solution. Moving freight by air connects cities, regions and countries around the world.

We are able to deliver flexible, innovative aviation logistics services throughout our global network, working with our customers to determine the best transport mode based on time, cost and freight type.

As well as general cargo, we can move a wide range of specialized freight by air including:

  • animals and pets
  • perishable and temperature-controlled goods
  • valuable cargo
  • fragile and sensitive goods
  • vehicles and parts

We also operate expedited freight services for time-critical consignments that require fast delivery.

SEA FREIGHT

Our diverse fleet and experience operating along the world’s major shipping lines enables us to deliver regular, secure and economical ocean freight services for our customers around the world.

Whether transporting smaller consignments or larger shipments across the ocean, our priority is to deliver your sea freight safely, on time and within budget.

In addition to international sea freight services, we also offer a range of coastal shipping operations to and from ports within the world.

Our sea freight services

  • Full container load (FCL) door-to-door import and export worldwide
  • Provision of containers and cargo equipment
  • Export and Import sea freight and less than a container load (LCL)
  • Coastal shipping

Freight types we move by sea

As well as general cargo, we can move a wide range of specialized freight by sea including:

  • perishable and temperature-controlled goods
  • vehicles and parts
  • hanging garments
  • oil and gas
  • bulk commodities

Freight sizes we move by sea

Our diverse fleet and history of sea freight logistics experience enables us to handle freight in a range of sizes, including:

  • pallet freight
  • break bulk and bulk freight
  • full container loads
  • Less than container loads
  • oversized cargo
