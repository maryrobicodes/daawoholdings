Construction Services


Design / Build

Daawo Holdings Limited simplifies complex building projects. By uniting owner, architect and contractor to create a unique team for each project. Our Design/Build program uses the fast track construction method which compresses the time required for the completion of a construction project as it permits the simultaneous execution of many construction activities. And that means lower interim financing costs and reduced financing risks.

Electrical Works

We have an extensive understanding of our clients’ electrical needs and we are able to plan accordingly. Whether you are rewiring an existing building or setting up new premises, we have the know-how to get it done right.

We plan for the future at Electrical Works and while wiring your building we will leave plenty of space to expand with more electrical features. This saves you time and money when you need to expand operations in the future.

We know we are not the cheapest commercial electricians out there, but we know our customers and our customers know that we recommend only the highest quality equipment and service.
