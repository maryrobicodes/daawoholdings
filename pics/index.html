About Us


Daawo Holdings Limited was established in Kenya in 2011 under the Company Act (Chapter 486 of the Law of Kenya).


Daawo Holdings Limited is located at Nairobi CBD dealing in transportation services, supplies and construction. The company also does transportation in Eastern and Central Africa. We are a customer driven and dedicated to providing affordable products and services while placing a strong emphasis on crediting long-term relationship with our clients.


Daawo Holdings Limited has a unique blend of complementary products and services which, when combined with the successful consulting and training methodologies provide an integrated strategic capability.
